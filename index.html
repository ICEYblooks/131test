1-3-1 Initiative

1 act of discipleship a day. 3 people to pray for who are far from God. 1 invite to church a week.

Examples of 1 Act of Discipleship

  • Encourage someone with a verse or prayer
  • Check in on a friend going through a hard time
  • Share your testimony with someone
  • Help meet a practical need for a neighbor
  • Invite someone to pray with you during lunch
